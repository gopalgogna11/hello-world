32-bit CPU microprocessor core, based on the SPARC-V8 RISC architecture and instruction set

Features
  • 1 AHB Master
  • 1 APBSlave
  • SlaveIntController for Interrupt Handeling
  • JTAG
    • Applications
  • used in system-on-a-chip (SOC) designs both in research and commercial settings.
